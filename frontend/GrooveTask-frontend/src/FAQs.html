Frequently Asked Questions

GrooveTask is the all-in-one platform designed to help you manage your tasks effectively while enjoying your favorite music. With GrooveTask, you can create, assign, and track tasks seamlessly, all while listening to your favorite tunes. Say goodbye to the hassle of task management and hello to productivity and enjoyment with GrooveTask.

To get started with GrooveTask, simply click the "Get Started" button on the top right corner of the page. You will be prompted to create an account and sign in. Once you have signed in, you can start creating, assigning, and tracking tasks seamlessly, all while enjoying your favorite tunes.

To add tasks to GrooveTask, simply click the "Add Task" button on the top right corner of the page. You will be prompted to enter the details of the task, including the title, description, due date, and assignee. Once you have entered the details, click "Save" to add the task to your list.

To assign tasks to others in GrooveTask, simply click the "Assign" button next to the task you want to assign. You will be prompted to select the assignee from your list of contacts. Once you have selected the assignee, click "Save" to assign the task to them.
